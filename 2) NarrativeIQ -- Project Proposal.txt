NARRATIVEIQ
Global Event & Narrative Intelligence Platform
1. Abstract

Modern society generates enormous volumes of digital information through news platforms, social media networks, discussion forums, blogs, and public communication channels. While traditional analytics systems focus on isolated metrics such as engagement, popularity, or sentiment, they often fail to capture the evolution of narratives that shape public opinion and influence decision-making.

NarrativeIQ is a Global Event and Narrative Intelligence Platform designed to transform fragmented digital information into structured intelligence. The system continuously collects data from multiple digital sources, performs large-scale data processing and enrichment, stores historical information within a dimensional data warehouse, and reconstructs the lifecycle of narratives over time.

The platform enables users to identify emerging events, analyze sentiment shifts, track influential entities, detect narrative evolution patterns, and forecast future trends. Through the integration of ETL pipelines, dimensional modeling, historical warehousing, business intelligence analytics, and predictive intelligence techniques, NarrativeIQ demonstrates a comprehensive application of Data Warehousing and Big Data principles.

The proposed system serves as an advanced intelligence platform capable of supporting researchers, analysts, media organizations, policy makers, and decision-makers by converting large-scale unstructured data into actionable narrative intelligence.

2. Introduction

The exponential growth of digital communication has fundamentally changed how information is produced, distributed, and consumed. Every day, millions of articles, posts, comments, discussions, and public reactions contribute to the formation of narratives surrounding major events.

Organizations increasingly require the ability to monitor not only what is being discussed but also how discussions evolve over time. Existing analytical systems primarily provide descriptive insights such as counts, trends, and summary statistics. However, they rarely offer mechanisms for understanding narrative formation, evolution, influence, and decline.

NarrativeIQ addresses this limitation by introducing a platform that combines Big Data processing techniques with Data Warehousing methodologies to create a historical intelligence system capable of reconstructing narrative evolution across multiple information sources.

3. Problem Statement

Digital information ecosystems generate vast amounts of heterogeneous and rapidly evolving data. News organizations, researchers, analysts, and decision-makers face significant challenges when attempting to understand:

How narratives emerge and evolve.
How public sentiment changes over time.
Which entities influence public discourse.
Which topics are gaining or losing relevance.
How information spreads across digital ecosystems.

Traditional analytics systems focus primarily on current metrics and isolated observations. They lack the capability to preserve historical narrative intelligence and provide long-term analytical insights regarding information evolution.

Consequently, organizations struggle to identify emerging trends, predict narrative shifts, and understand the lifecycle of public discourse.
